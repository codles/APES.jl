[method] —
apes{T<:FloatingPoint}(y::Array{T<:FloatingPoint,N},f::Union(Array{T<:FloatingPoint,N},T<:FloatingPoint))

Amplitude and Phase Estimation of a Sinusoid (APES). Implemented as described in Li, Li & Stoica 1998.

This function estimates the complex amplitude of signal y at frequencies f. M denotes the filter length, which is set to N/2 by default [Li & Stoica 1996].
